Gaurav's 24-Hour Schedule

Morning

  • 6:00 AM - 6:30 AM: Wake up and morning routine (freshen up, light stretching).
  • 6:30 AM - 7:00 AM: Meditation or a short morning walk for a fresh start.
  • 7:00 AM - 8:00 AM: Exercise or physical activity (strength training, yoga, or jogging).
  • 8:00 AM - 8:30 AM: Breakfast and relax.

Learning Block 1 - (4)

  • 8:30 AM - 11:30 AM: Data Structures & Algorithms (DSA) - Focus on solving 2-3 LeetCode questions.
  • 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM: College Study - Focus on current semester subjects.

Midday

  • 12:30 PM - 1:30 PM: Lunch and relax.
  • 1:30 PM - 2:00 PM: Power nap or light reading.

Learning Block 2 - (3)

  • 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM: Web Development - Learn/revise frontend concepts and work on projects.
  • 5:00 PM - 5:30 PM: Break with snacks or a short walk.

Evening - (1.5)

  • 5:30 PM - 7:00 PM: Coding Practice/Project Work - Solve 1-2 LeetCode problems or enhance projects.
  • 7:00 PM - 8:00 PM: Dinner and family time.

Learning Block 3 - (1.5)

  • 8:00 PM - 9:30 PM: College Studies - Focus on current semester subjects (e.g., DSA with C++, Networking Technologies, OOPS, etc.).
  • 9:30 PM - 10:00 PM: Short break (listen to music, relax).

Late Evening - (1)

  • 10:00 PM - 11:00 PM: Daily Revision - Review key learnings and plan for the next day.
  • 11:00 PM - 11:30 PM: Wind down (light reading or journaling).

Night

  • 11:30 PM - 6:00 AM: Sleep.
